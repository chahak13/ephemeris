Min of 2 courses from each of the 3 areas A, B, C. Also, minimum of 24 credits from at least one of the three areas. (24??, need to confirm that). No other "requirements" per se.

Discussion of course options by Graduate Advisor

CSEM Course Inventory is all the courses that are particularly CSEM (prefix CSE). Approved courses is a list of all courses. Also, 9 credit hours are required for full-time enrollments which is more than required credits for Thesis or Report options. In such a case, we need to contact ISSS for an exception.
